Sour Cream Pancakes

You'll have to guess the amounts, but this is a favorite of mine that my mom brought back from hawaii!

  • Sour cream
  • flour
  • eggs
  • sugar
  • baking soda
  • salt
  1. Begin by mixing all ingredients into a large mixing bowl, and mix until smooth.
  2. Chill overnight
  3. Spoon batter onto hot greased griddle and cook 3 minutes per side
  4. Serve fresh with strawberries
Voila!
