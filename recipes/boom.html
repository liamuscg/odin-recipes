Boom Sauce

This is an extremely simple recipe, but makes for a delicious aioli that can be spread on a sandwich or used as a dip

  • mayonaise
  • sweet chili sauce
  • sriracha
  1. Begin by mixing two parts mayo with one part sweet chili sauce
  2. squeeze in a dash of sriracha (be careful, it packs a punch if you're white)
  3. Enjoy!
